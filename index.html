Design records

SELECTED ARCHITECTURE WORK
DR-014

Subscription vending for business units

Problem
Business units waited weeks for compliant environments, and every subscription looked different.
Design
Self-service vending pipeline in Terraform: networking, policy, RBAC, and budgets stamped in on day zero.
Outcome
Environment lead time cut from weeks to hours, with governance built in rather than bolted on.
DR-021

Policy-driven governance at scale

Problem
Manual reviews couldn't keep pace with dozens of teams deploying independently.
Design
Layered Azure Policy across management groups — deny where it matters, audit where teams need freedom.
Outcome
Compliance shifted from gatekeeping to guardrails; exceptions became data, not arguments.
DR-027

Hybrid connectivity redesign

Problem
Growing traffic between plants, offices, and cloud strained a first-generation network design.
Design
Consolidated hub with tiered firewall rules, private DNS, and clear spoke onboarding standards.
Outcome
Predictable connectivity for every new landing zone — onboarding a spoke is now a checklist, not a project.
